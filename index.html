//THE EXHIBIT

Make your work consistent but not fucking predictable. Think about all the fucking possibilities. A good fucking composition is the result of a hierarchy consisting of clearly contrasting elements set with distinct alignments containing irregular intervals of negative space. When you sit down to work, external critics aren’t the enemy. It’s you who you must to fight against to do great fucking work. You must overcome yourself. This design is fucking brilliant. Someday is not a fucking day of the week. Sometimes it is appropriate to place various typographic elements on the outside of the fucking left margin of text to maintain a strong vertical axis. This practice is referred to as exdenting and is most often used with bullets and quotations. When you design, you have to draw on your own fucking life.
