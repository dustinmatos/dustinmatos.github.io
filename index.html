//THE EXHIBIT

Automaton is an exhibit focused on the past and present of robotics and technology, as well as video games and movies. We as designers wanted to contrast the past with the present and did so by separating these two different eras onto two different floors. The first floor explores older robotics and technologies while the second floor displays some interesting advances in facial recognition and touchscreen software.
